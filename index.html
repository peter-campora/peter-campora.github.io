Background

I'm a fourth year PhD student at the University of Louisiana at Lafayette who is studying programming language theory under Dr. Sheng Chen. My primary research focus has been using variational typing as a means to analyze the properties of gradually typed programs foruse in the creation of tools aiding program migration. I also have a broad interest in making Gradual Typing easier to implement and use.
